Title:
------

Stabilize Me!



Description:
------------

Allows stabilization of a soldier that's bleeding out by any other soldier, provided that the soldier that's bleeding out has a Medikit. 

No more bleeding out deaths for your medics! As long as the incapacitated soldier with the Medikit has at least one charge left he can be stabilized by anyone.

This mod is open source. The source along with documentation is available in Github, https://github.com/Jusas/XCOM2StabilizeMe
